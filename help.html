Help & Support

Need help? We've got you covered! Find answers to common questions and get the support you need.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, click "Add to Cart" on items you want, then click the cart icon and proceed to checkout. It's that simple!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay for a secure checkout experience.

How much is delivery?

Delivery is free on orders over ¬£50. For orders under ¬£50, standard delivery is ¬£4.99. Express delivery (next day) is available for ¬£9.99.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 working days. Express delivery arrives the next working day. We'll send you tracking information once your order ships.

Can I return items?

Yes! We offer a 30-day return policy. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Simply contact us to arrange a return.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can also check your order status in your account dashboard.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within the UK. We're working on international shipping and will announce it soon!

How do I change my account details?

Log into your account and go to "Account Settings" to update your personal information, delivery address, and payment methods.
